## Part 1

Sketches
1. **Design challenge**: Spiel designen das Gebrauch von den mobilen Kontexten macht
2. Generate 10+ different design concepts that address that challenge
3. Reduce number of design concepts OR repeat
4. Choose most promising concepts
5. Product 10 details/variations of choosen concepts (1-2)
6. Present your ideas to a group
7. Settle on one idea and make 10 sketches to that as a group
8. As your ideas change, sketch them out
